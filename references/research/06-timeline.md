## Latest status

As of 2026-06-02, Warsh is the chairman of the Federal Reserve Board and chairman of the FOMC. His Board term ends 2040-01-31; his chair term ends 2030-05-21.

## Recent live constraints

- Inflation has remained a politically salient issue.
- The Fed's independence is under close scrutiny because President Trump has publicly favored lower rates.
- Balance-sheet policy, communication framework, and Fed-Treasury boundary questions are likely early credibility tests.
- New FOMC decisions after 2026-06-02 are not covered by this research snapshot and must be checked with current sources.
